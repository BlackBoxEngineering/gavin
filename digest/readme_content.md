# Content & Messaging

## About Gavin (source: gwoo.co.uk — to be refined)

Gavin Woodhouse built a group of businesses from 2013 that by 2019 was turning over more than £40 million per annum, employing over 450 staff across multiple UK locations. Independent valuations projected potential into the hundreds of millions.

In 2019 he was subjected to a coordinated campaign of slander and manipulated media coverage involving prominent UK press, a law firm, and a global insolvency business. The fallout was severe and public — affecting every area of his life: mental, physical, financial, and personal.

He has since rebuilt and now offers mentoring and strategic advisory services to business owners — specifically focused on navigating crisis, potential downfall, and the circumstances that most advisors are unwilling to engage with.

## Core Positioning
- "The mentor who stays when others run"
- Speaks from lived experience of public failure and recovery
- Targets business owners facing or wanting to prevent catastrophic risk

## Key Messages
1. He built something significant — credibility is real
2. What happened to him was done *to* him — not by him
3. He has navigated the worst and come out the other side
4. He offers something no conventional advisor can: presence in the darkest moments
